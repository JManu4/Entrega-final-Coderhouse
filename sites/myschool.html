Morelia Institute of Technology is a public educational institution of higher education, which is part of the National System of Technological Institutes of Mexico.

Located in Morelia city, Michoacán, this educational institution owns great prestige and tradition, holds first place in the regional ranking of high-performance Technological Institutes.
